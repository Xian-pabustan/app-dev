## Main Characters
- **Percy Jackson**: The protagonist and son of Poseidon.
- **Annabeth Chase**: Daughter of Athena and Percy's ally.
- **Grover Underwood**: A satyr and Percy's protector.
- **Luke Castellan**: A demigod who plays a crucial role in the story.
